11268 Discmania - MD1 d-line - Midrange/Control

MD1 er den første Discmania golf disc. Den har en stor diameter og er en stabil mellemdistance disc med et meget pålideligt svæv og langsomt fade.

The MD1 is the first Discmania Golf Disc (PDGA approved). MD1 is a large diameter, 180 gram max weight midrange driver. It is a stable flyer with good range and a predictable low speed fade. The MD1 comes with two blends: more grippier D-line and very durable S-line. Weight range is from 168 to 180 gram.

  • Producent: Discmania
  • Plast: D-line
  • Diameter: 21,7cm
  • Technic: Speed 4, Glide 4, Turn 0, Fade 2
